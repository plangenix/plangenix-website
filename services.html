Tender Programming

A well-prepared tender program is one of the most powerful tools in your bid. It demonstrates your methodology, shows the Principal you've thought through every phase of delivery, and gives evaluators confidence that you can actually do what you're proposing.

We prepare logic-driven, credible tender programs that align with your methodology statement and commercial strategy — without over-engineering. Tender programs at this stage don't need resource loading; they need to be clear, achievable, and compelling.

Construction Programming

Your contract baseline program is the document that governs your project's time performance. It needs to be contractually compliant, logically sound, and actually useful — not just a document to satisfy the Superintendent.

We develop detailed construction programs that are logic-driven, structured to support contract administration, and built to be updated throughout the project lifecycle. From Level 2 phase programs through to detailed Level 3–4 works programs.

Contract baseline program — Level 2, 3, or 4
Logic-driven activity sequences
Key date and milestone tracking
3-week lookahead programs
Program revisions and update management

Typical Program Levels

Level 2 — Phase Program
Major work packages and key dependencies
Level 3 — Works Program (Most common)
Full activity-level schedule with logic and durations
Level 4 — Detailed Works Program
Granular sequencing for complex or high-risk scopes

Project Controls

Without proper controls, you're managing a project by feel — and by the time a problem becomes visible, it's often too late to recover. We implement project controls frameworks that give you genuine early warning and real decision-making capability.

Our project controls services are calibrated to your project type, contract form, and reporting requirements — from simple schedule updates to full EVM-integrated cost and schedule performance tracking.
