About PlanGenix

Planning. Precision.
Passion for Programs.

PlanGenix is a Sydney-based scheduling and project controls consultancy built on over 12 years of hands-on experience in Australian construction and infrastructure.

The Founder

Mandip Desai

I'm a Planner and Master Scheduler with over 12 years of experience delivering project schedules and project controls across government, infrastructure, defence, health, and commercial sectors throughout Australia.

My career includes roles as a Project Control Analyst at Sydney Trains, Project Planner at Thales on the HMAS DDG Fleet, and Estimator and Planner at Aurify Constructions, where I ran tenders and managed construction programmes end to end.

For the past few years I've also been working as a freelance planner with Plannery, one of Australia's most active tender programming firms, preparing programs for contractors across NSW, VIC, QLD, WA and the ACT. Education, health, aged care, community, government and commercial have all been part of the mix.

PlanGenix is where all of that comes together. A focused practice where I work directly with contractors, developers and principals who need solid scheduling done properly and on time.

Where I've Worked

Project Control Analyst

Sydney Trains

Working across major infrastructure programs including the Digital Systems Transformation, Rail Timetable System and Rail Service Integration Program. Building and maintaining integrated master schedules in P6, running schedule performance reporting and critical path analysis, and supporting project managers with programme governance across the portfolio.

Project Planner & Scheduler

Thales

Planner on the HMAS DDG Fleet project, building and maintaining P6 schedules, conducting EVM reviews, developing Power BI dashboards for cost, time and resource reporting, and integrating subcontractor programs into the master schedule. Freelance Planner

Plannery & Independent Clients

Preparing tender programs for builders across Australia, working through Plannery and on direct engagements. The mix of work covers education, health, aged care, community, government, commercial and residential projects in NSW, VIC, QLD, WA and the ACT. It's been a great way to stay sharp across a lot of different project types and procurement requirements.

Estimator / Planner / Project Manager
